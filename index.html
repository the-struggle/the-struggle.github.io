The Struggle

A year-long adventure moving from academia to industry and exploring automated trading at home.

Hi! I'm Tyler. I'm a grad student who is (hopefully) about to graduate and am transitioning to the financial industry. I'm very interested in algorithmic trading, but information is very sparse and I've struggled a lot to gather my thoughts on how to get started. This blog is a place for me to aggregate everything I learn through this struggle. I commit to post every day, Monday through Friday, something I've learned about algorithmic trading. Hopefully, by this time next year, this blog will be a place where you can understand every step of the process, from strategy to software to brokers to performance evaluation.

A word to the wise: this is pretty much just stream of consciousness/unedited fumbling about. I'm not taking it too seriously and neither should you!
