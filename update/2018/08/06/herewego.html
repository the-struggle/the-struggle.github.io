Here We Go!

Aug 6, 2018

That’s it folks! I’ve gotten this somewhat sloppy website up and running! Over the coming year I plan to write here once a day as a way to motivate myself to actually keep up with my algorithmic trading work. In particular, what goals do I want to achieve?

I want to have a running trading algorithm that is trading flat (or better) for at least one month by September of 2019.

I consider this to be a lofty goal. Here’s a list of some things I can think of that I want to touch on during the year:

  • Strategies: An algorithm is only as good as the strategy behind it.
  • Backtesting: We need to try to know how our strategy would have done if we were trading. This is a huge topic and will probably be the bulk of the work.
  • Brokers: What brokers offer API services for what kinds of instruments? What are the costs? How do we incorporate this API into our code?
  • Execution: Practically speaking, how can we execute trades automatically? How do we implement stop loss? How do we tell the difference between market movements and algorithm failure?
  • Performance Tracking: Once we are trading, what information is pertinent to be able to analyze our performance? What tools exist to do this kind of performance analysis?

Some of these are broader than others and will require on-going study, while others are more precise and once discussed can be set aside for a while. I’ll be back tomorrow with a first real post!
